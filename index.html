Please read this agreement carefully. By signing, you agree to all terms stated herein.

💰 Payment Terms — Articles 1 & 2

Article 1. Reservation Fee

  • Non-refundable Reservation Fee of PHP 2,000 upon signing.
  • Deducted from the total package fee upon the event.
  • Date is released if payment is not received within 24 hours.

Article 2. Full Payment

  • Remaining balance must be settled in full at least three (3) days before the event date.
  • Accepted methods: GCash, bank transfer, or cash.

Section A — Client & Event Information

Section B — Package & Payment Summary

Articles 3–5: Scheduling & Cancellation

Art. 3 Date Change: One (1) date change allowed, PHP 500 rebooking fee, request 10 days before event.

Art. 4 Cancellation: 15+ days: forfeit reservation fee. 7–14 days: 50% forfeited. Within 7 days / no-show: 100% forfeited.

Art. 5 Force Majeure: Rescheduled at no penalty. No cash refund; rescheduling credit applies.

Articles 6–8: Venue Use & Time

Art. 6 Hours: Total 7 hours = 1hr Ingress + 1hr Free Bonus + 4hr Event Proper + 1hr Egress.

Art. 7 Overtime: PHP 1,500/hour. Request 30 min before event ends. Subject to availability.

Art. 8 Capacity: Maximum 100 persons. Overcrowding = grounds for event suspension without refund.

Articles 9–12: Property Care & Damage

Art. 9 Cleanliness: Venue must be returned in same clean condition. Failure = forfeiture of cleaning deposit.

Art. 10 Cleaning Deposit: PHP 1,000 refundable. Returned within 3 banking days if no issues.

Art. 11 Property Damage: Client liable for all damages. Costs assessed post-event, payable within 7 days.

Art. 12 Prohibited Acts: No illegal substances, smoking, nails/tape on walls, open flames, exceeding capacity, or violent conduct.

Articles 13–14: Authority & Disputes

Art. 13 Manager Authority: Presencia has full authority to enforce rules, suspend or terminate events for violations.

Art. 14 Dispute Resolution: Good-faith negotiation first, then mediation. Philippine law governs. Venue: Taguig City.

For full legal enforceability, both parties are encouraged to have this Agreement notarized by a licensed Notary Public in Taguig City.
