TERMS AND CONDITIONS

Please read the following terms and conditions carefully before signing this agreement.

Articles 1 to 8 — Payment, Scheduling, Cancellation, and Venue Use

PAYMENT TERMS
Article 1. Reservation Fee

The Client shall pay a non-refundable Reservation Fee of PHP 2,000 upon signing. This amount is deducted from the total package fee upon the event. The event date is considered reserved only upon receipt of this fee. Presencia reserves the right to release the date if payment is not received within 24 hours of booking confirmation.

Article 2. Full Payment

The remaining balance must be settled in full at least three (3) days before the event date. Venue access will not be granted without confirmed full payment clearance. Accepted payment methods: GCash, bank transfer, or cash — as agreed with the Venue Manager.

SCHEDULING & CANCELLATION
Article 3. Date Change Policy

The Client may request one (1) date change, subject to venue availability. A rebooking fee of PHP 500 applies to all approved date change requests. Requests must be submitted in writing at least 10 days before the original event date. Requests submitted within 7 days of the event will not be accommodated.

Article 4. Cancellation Policy
  • Cancellation 15 or more days before the event: Reservation fee is forfeited. The remaining balance, if already paid, shall be refunded.
  • Cancellation 7 to 14 days before the event: 50% of the total package rate is forfeited. No refund will be issued on this amount.
  • Cancellation within 7 days or no-show on event day: 100% of the total package rate is forfeited. No refund will be issued.

All cancellations must be submitted in writing to presenciavenue@gmail.com.

Article 5. Force Majeure

In the event of a natural disaster, government-mandated closure, or any circumstance beyond reasonable control, affected bookings shall be rescheduled at no penalty to either party. No cash refund shall be issued under force majeure. A rescheduling credit shall apply.

VENUE USE & TIME
Article 6. Venue Use Hours

All packages include a total of 7 hours, allocated as follows:

  • 1 Hour — Ingress: Venue setup and decoration
  • 1 Hour — Free Bonus Hour: Additional preparation or buffer time
  • 4 Hours — Event Proper: Main celebration or function
  • 1 Hour — Egress: Cleanup and pack-up

The event must fully conclude and the venue vacated within the agreed time.

Article 7. Extension / Overtime

Any extension beyond the agreed hours is charged at PHP 1,500 per hour. Extension requests must be made at least 30 minutes before the event ends. Presencia reserves the right to deny extension if the venue has a subsequent booking.

Article 8. Venue Capacity

The maximum allowed number of guests is 100 persons at any given time. The Client is responsible for ensuring their guest list does not exceed this limit. Overcrowding that compromises safety is grounds for immediate event suspension without refund.

Articles 9 to 14 — Property Care, Damage Policy & Acknowledgment

The Client agrees to protect and respect the venue at all times.

PROPERTY CARE & CLEANLINESS
Article 9. Cleanliness Obligation

The Client is fully responsible for maintaining the cleanliness of the venue throughout the event. Upon egress, the venue must be returned in the same clean and orderly condition as received. The following must be completed before the Client vacates the premises:

  • All trash, food waste, and decorations must be collected and properly disposed of.
  • Tables, chairs, and furniture must be returned to their original arrangement.
  • No food, drinks, or personal belongings shall be left inside the venue.
  • Floors must be free from spills, stains, and debris.

Failure to meet cleanliness standards shall result in full forfeiture of the Cleaning Deposit.

Article 10. Cleaning Deposit

A refundable Cleaning Deposit of PHP 1,000 is required upon signing this Agreement. The deposit is returned within three (3) banking days after the event, provided:

  • The venue is returned clean and in its original condition.
  • No property damage has been identified during post-event inspection.

Presencia will provide photographic documentation of any cleanliness issues as basis for withholding.

Article 11. Property Damage Policy

The Client is fully liable for any damage to the venue, furniture, fixtures, equipment, walls, flooring, or property within the premises caused by the Client, guests, or their suppliers. Damage costs shall be assessed immediately after the event by Presencia Venue Management. The Client agrees to pay all assessed damage costs in full within seven (7) days. The Cleaning Deposit shall be applied first toward damages. If costs exceed the deposit, the Client shall pay the remaining balance separately. Intentional damage or vandalism shall be subject to applicable legal action under Philippine law. All damages shall be documented with dated photographs as official evidence.

Article 12. Prohibited Acts Inside the Venue

The following are strictly prohibited and are grounds for immediate event termination without refund:

  • Bringing or using illegal substances or prohibited items inside the venue.
  • Smoking anywhere within the venue premises.
  • Hammering nails, using strong tape, or affixing anything directly to walls without written approval.
  • Use of open flames, fireworks, or pyrotechnics inside the venue.
  • Exceeding the maximum guest capacity of 100 persons.
  • Acts of violence, harassment, or conduct that disturbs neighboring areas.
AUTHORITY & DISPUTE RESOLUTION
Article 13. Manager Authority

Presencia Venue Management, as the authorized Venue Manager, has full authority to:

  • Enforce all venue rules and terms of this Agreement during the event.
  • Suspend or terminate the event immediately if any clause is violated.
  • Retain the Cleaning Deposit and pursue additional damage claims as applicable.
Article 14. Dispute Resolution

Any dispute shall first be resolved through good-faith negotiation between both parties. If unresolved within fifteen (15) days, the matter shall proceed to mediation. Philippine law governs this Agreement. Venue for proceedings: Taguig City.

ACKNOWLEDGMENT & SIGNATURES
